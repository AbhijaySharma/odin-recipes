Chicken Wings

Description

Recipe for delicous, savory air-fried buffalo chicken wings. Takes about 30 minutes and makes 2-3 servings of wings. You will need an air fryer for this. Great for a lunch, dinner, or even a snack!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of chicken wings
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup Frank's Original Red Hot Sauce
  • 4 tbsp unsalted melted butter

Steps

  1. Wash chicken wings under hot water
  2. Preheat air fryer to 390°F
  3. Add 2 pounds of chicken wings to mixing bowl
  4. Add salt, garlic powder, onion powder, and baking powder
  5. Mix thoroughly
  6. Using tongs, put 1 layer of wings in the air fryer, with space between each wing
  7. Cook wings for 12 minutes, flipping halfway through
  8. Cook wings for another 8 minutes at 400°F
  9. For sauce, mix 4 tbsp butter and 1/2 cup Frank's Red Hot
  10. Add wings and sauce to mixing bowl
  11. Toss and serve
