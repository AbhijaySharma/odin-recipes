Pepperoni Pizza

Description

Recipe for a delicous, cheesy pepperoni pizza. Uses some store-bought ingredients, so not completely from scratch. Takes about 25 minutes to prep and cook, but yields about 8 servings of goodness. You will need a cast-iron skillet and an oven. Good for a quick dinner/lunch and leads to great leftovers.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz. of store-bought pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup of store-bought pizza sauce
  • 18-20 slices of pepperoni
  • 12 oz. grated mozzerella cheese
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp fresh oregano
  • Flour for molding dough

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 500Â°F
  2. On a lightly floured surface, roll out dough to about a 14 inch diameter
  3. Fit the dough into a cast-iron skillet
  4. Add sauce lightly onto the dough, leaving a 1/4 inch gap at the edges
  5. Slice half of the pepperoni and put it on the sauce
  6. Top with grated cheese, and then the rest of the unsliced pepperoni. Season with black pepper
  7. Place the skillet over a high heat burner for 2 minutes
  8. Transfer to preheated oven and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until crust is golden brown
  9. Take out pizza and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing
  10. Season with oregano and serve
